Interest in IT

Having been playing games for nearly all of my life, I have wanted to join the I.T field since the age of 12. Watching the world, especially Tv’s revolution in front of me, has fascinated me. Going from having to use a bresom to manually clean your floor to having some A.I powered vacuum to do it for you with just a click on your phone or even voice automation surprised me. It wasn't until I had come to Australia that I decided Machine Learning/A.I was the field for me. Also having a particular interest in software engineering, I am certain that this is the path I'll take for a better future. Being an asian you're kind of hypnotized in a way to follow a career from either the Medical or Engineering field. Any other field is regarded as “lower” in many Asian, especially south asian countries. I myself experienced this same issue where my mum had wanted me to be a doctor, however I lacked the ability to just throw away my life just to earn top dollar. My dad on the other hand, having been a HR Manager, always told me to choose a path that not only would earn you a decent amount but also one that has a high growth chance in the future. Him having studied human behavior and all its aspects he has nurtured me alot in regards to work and its environments. It wasn’t until recently that my mum had realized that her kid won't be a doctor, however she still seems to occasionally remind me that I could have been one. I currently lack experience however i have created websites in the past and even made a flappy bird prototype in year 12. Doing this course, I aim to enhance my growth and gain more experience. Why i chose to come to RMIT? I was stuck between choosing RMIT or Monash, however after going through the curriculum and course overview I chose RMIT. I even discussed it with my secondary school teachers and they had suggested that I choose RMIT. The word “Artificial Intelligence” on the course website was more than enough to attract me to this course. As I stated previously, this is the field I was interested in. Although Monash also offered A.I, it required Math Methods in Year 12 which I did not do. Furthermore I'm expecting to learn handson experience that could easily lead me to a path in the future. I am expecting to learn various fields of I.T and how they interact with each other on a daily basis. Not only that, I also expect to learn how I.T could pave the way for a more optimistic future.
